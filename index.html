About Me

Hi, I'm Ravindar — a software engineer passionate about the frontier of agentic AI. I love exploring how autonomous agents can collaborate to solve complex, creative problems that no single model could handle alone.

My recent work focuses on multi-agent orchestration: building master agents that coordinate specialized workers for storytelling, image generation, text-to-speech, music composition, and video assembly. I enjoy taking cutting-edge AI capabilities and wiring them together into practical, working systems.

When I'm not building agents, I'm experimenting with new models and frameworks, and sharing what I learn through open-source projects on GitHub.
